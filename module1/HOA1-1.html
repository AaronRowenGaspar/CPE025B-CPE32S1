Valencia VC202TBU 200 series Classical Guitar - Transparent Blue (1/2 size)

The Valencia VC202TBU 200 series Classical Guitar - Transparent Blue (1/2 size) is manufactured to Valencia's exacting standards in their own factory in Indonesia and starts with a Sitka spruce top, nato body and jabon neck for balanced tone. A distinctive, see-through satin finish reveals the inherent beauty of the used tonewoods, while ebonized mahogany fingerboard and bridge with plastic saddle completes the elegant look. Both neck and top feature black ABS binding. The neck provides 19 brass frets and side dot inlay. Scale length is 533 mm, nut width is 48 mm. Other features include a Valencia 'Bat' motif rosette and nickel-plated machine heads with cream buttons. Transparent Blue colour design.
